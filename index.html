Why Barcelona?

From beaches to mountains, Barcelona offers a lot to see and do. Beautiful surrounding landscapes, stunning architecture and diverse culture. Barcelona is great for its art, history and it's nightlife. If you're into skateboarding you'll know it is well know for its skate spots. You will never be bored, visiting or living here.

If you love landscapes and nature there are plenty of places accessible by public transport, surrounding Barcelona to seek a great view or some peace from the busy streets.
