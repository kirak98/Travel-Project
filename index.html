Why Barcelona?

From beaches to mountains, Barcelona offers a lot to see and do. Beautiful surrounding landscapes, stunning architecture and diverse culture. Barcelona is great for its art, history and it's nightlife. If you're into skateboarding you'll know it is well know for its skate spots. You will never be bored, visiting or living here.

If you love landscapes and nature there are plenty of places accessible by public transport, surrounding Barcelona to seek a great view or some peace from the busy streets.

Viewpoints of Barcelona

El Tibidabo

Tibidabo - Barcelona's highest peak, with beautiful surrounding hikes and sites to explore. Temple Expiatori del Sagrat Cor de Jesús and Parc d'atraccions Tibidabo.

Address:

El Tibidabo, 08035 Barcelona

Learn more

Montjuïc

Montjuïc has lots of hidden gems, from a view of Plaça Espanya to Tibidabo, musuems, the Castle of Barcelona, the Olympic stadium and more...

Address:

Montjuïc, 08038 Barcelona

Learn more

Montserrat

Just outside of Barcelona, Montserrat is a multipeaked mountain range, best know for its natural parks, many hiking trails and a wide range of wildlife...

Address:

Montserrat, 08691, Barcelona
